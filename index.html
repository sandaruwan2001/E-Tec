About E‑Tec

E‑Tec International is an ICT class for all levels — from Nursery to A/L — in both English and Sinhala mediums. We focus on practical, exam-ready skills and real-world projects.

  • Nursery, Grade 1–5, Grade 6–O/L, A/L
  • Graphic Design, Office Package (NVQ), ICT short courses
  • Student portal for progress and marks
  • Admin panel to manage courses, events, and results
